Gnocchi with Bolognese

Description:

Our Gnocchi with Bolognese is a hearty and comforting dish, perfect for those seeking classic Italian flavors. Soft and pillowy potato gnocchi is smothered in a rich and flavorful Bolognese sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, carrots, and aromatic herbs. Topped with a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings and warm your soul.

Ingredients:

  • 500g potato gnocchi
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely diced
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  2. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and diced carrot to the skillet. Cook until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the gnocchi according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. Once the Bolognese sauce is ready, add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet. Toss until the gnocchi is coated evenly with the sauce.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
